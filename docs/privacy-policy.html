Privacy Policy

App: claudulhu  ·  Last updated: March 2026

claudulhu is a mobile interface for connecting to a self-hosted coding agent server. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Data We Collect

We collect no data. claudulhu does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information with the app developer or any third party.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores one item locally on your device:

  • Server address — the IP address or hostname of your claudulhu server. This is saved using your device's local storage so you don't have to re-enter it each time. It never leaves your device except as the destination of your own network connections.

3. Network Connections

The app connects directly to the server address you provide over your local network or the internet. All messages you send are transmitted to your own server — not to the developer of this app. The app makes no connections to any servers operated by the developer.

Your server may in turn communicate with the Anthropic API to power the AI assistant. That communication is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy, and is outside the scope of this app.

4. Analytics & Tracking

The app contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or any other tracking technology.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a revised date at the top.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer via the contact information listed on the app's Play Store page.
