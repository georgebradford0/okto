## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Okto?

Okto is a mobile client for connecting to your own self-hosted Okto "lair"
server. It is a thin client — it talks only to a server you control and does not
collect personal data. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for details.

### How do I connect to my server?

Open the app and scan the QR code shown by your lair server, or enter the
connection details (host, port, and public key) manually. Camera access is only
used to scan the QR code.

### Why can't the app connect to my server?

A few things to check:

- Make sure your lair server is running and reachable from your device's network.
- Confirm the host, port, and public key match what your server is advertising.
- If you recently re-created the server, re-scan the latest QR code, as the
  public key may have changed.

### How do I clear my data?

Logging out within the app clears the locally stored connection details, message
drafts, and cached chat history from your device.

### Is my data private?

Yes. All communication with your server is end-to-end encrypted, and the app
contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising. See the full
[Privacy Policy](privacy.md).
